Fellow of the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Doctoral Network LongTREC, in collaboration with Ana Conesa (I2SysBio).

Thesis: *Dimension Reduction on Time Series Data*. Applied PCA, t-SNE, UMAP, and an autoencoder (TensorFlow/Keras) to anomaly detection in clinical time-series data.

Thesis: *Towards Predicting the Popularity of Music Artists*. Presented at the 10th International Workshop on Machine Learning and Music at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona.
